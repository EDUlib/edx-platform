Established in 1873, Polytechnique Montréal is one of the most important engineering faculties and research institutions in Canada. Polytechnique ranks first in Quebec in terms of student body and the scope of its research activities. With more than 44,300 graduates, Polytechnique Montréal has educated nearly a quarter of the current members of the Quebec Order of Engineers. The institution offers more than 120 programs to some 8,000 students taught by 255 professors. Its annual operating budget is over $200 million, including dedicated research resources of more than $70 million.
